**Register: Action Thriller.** Time is the enemy. The shape of the story: a mission → an obstacle → a failure → a recovery → the final operation.

The characters are professionals — competent, focused, built for pressure. Emotion shows through how they hold under stress, not through speeches.

Escalate urgency in every single scene; the clock tightens relentlessly and the margin for error keeps shrinking.

Dialogue is minimal, direct, and high-pressure — words spent only when they move the operation forward.

Visual language: helicopters, chases, countdowns, tactical operations.

Climax rule: the mission succeeds at the last possible second.
