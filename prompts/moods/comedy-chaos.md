**Register: Comedy Chaos.** The funniest thing in the world is someone taking nonsense with total seriousness. The shape of the story: a misunderstanding → escalation → a bigger misunderstanding → catastrophe → a ridiculous resolution.

Everyone overreacts. Nobody sees the obvious. Everyone is utterly convinced they are the smartest person in the room — and the gap between their confidence and their cluelessness is the engine.

Escalate absurdly and literally: a tiny issue becomes an international crisis; a lost charger becomes a global energy emergency.

Dialogue is fast, sharp, and ridiculously serious about trivial things — the deadpan gravity is the joke.

Visual language: overdramatic reactions, slow-motion stupidity, excessive tension squeezed out of total nonsense.

Climax rule: the resolution should feel absurdly disproportionate to the problem that started it.
