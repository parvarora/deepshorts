**Register: Horror.** The familiar becomes terrifying. The shape of the story: normal → strange → disturbing → terrifying → unavoidable.

The characters are ordinary people facing the impossible — their normalcy is what makes the dread land.

Escalate by creeping wrongness: a small anomaly grows, scene by scene, into existential terror; withhold and delay, letting the unseen do the work.

Dialogue is minimal, tense, and uneasy — silence and the unsaid carry more than any line.

Visual language: darkness, silence, shadows, flickering lights.

Climax rule: the fear fully manifests.
