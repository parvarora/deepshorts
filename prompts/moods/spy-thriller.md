**Register: Spy Thriller.** Trust nobody. The shape of the story: a mystery → a conspiracy → a double-cross → a reveal → an even bigger conspiracy underneath.

Everyone has secrets. Nobody is fully honest. Loyalties are instruments, and every alliance is provisional.

Escalate by paranoia: every answer creates two new questions, and every door opens onto a larger room.

Dialogue is subtext-heavy; people rarely answer directly, and what's withheld carries more than what's said.

Visual language: surveillance, safehouses, encrypted screens, hidden files.

Climax rule: reveal the hidden architect behind everything.
