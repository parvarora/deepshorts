**Register: Tragic Masterpiece.** Everything meaningful costs something. The shape of the story: hope → attachment → sacrifice → loss → acceptance.

The characters are deeply human, deeply flawed, and deeply loved — so that whatever they lose, the audience loses with them.

Escalate by reversal: success becomes painful, and victory turns bittersweet; the closer they come to what they want, the more it costs.

Dialogue is beautiful pain — restrained, aching, and precise; the feeling sits just under the words.

Visual language: quiet, elegiac imagery; stillness where spectacle would be; light that fades.

Climax rule: the audience cries.
